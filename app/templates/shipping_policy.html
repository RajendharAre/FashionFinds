Shipping Costs

We offer competitive shipping rates with free shipping thresholds:

  • Free Standard Shipping: On all orders over $50
  • Standard Shipping: $5.99 for orders under $50
  • Express Shipping: $9.99 flat rate
  • Overnight Shipping: $19.99 flat rate
  • International Shipping: Calculated at checkout based on destination

Note

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your delivery address and selected shipping method. Some oversized or heavy items may incur additional shipping charges.

Return Shipping Procedures

If you need to return an item, we make the process as simple as possible:

Initiating a Return

  1. Log into your account and go to "My Orders"
  2. Select the order containing the item(s) you want to return
  3. Click "Request Return" and follow the prompts
  4. Print the prepaid return label (for domestic returns)
  5. Package the item securely and attach the return label
  6. Drop off at any authorized shipping location

Important Information

  • Return shipping is free for domestic orders within 30 days of purchase
  • International return shipping is the customer's responsibility
  • Original shipping costs are non-refundable
  • Items must be in original condition with tags attached
