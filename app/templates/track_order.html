How Order Tracking Works

At Fashion Finds, we believe in transparency. Here's how you can track your order from placement to delivery:

Order Confirmation

Once you place an order, you'll receive an email confirmation with your order details and estimated processing time.

Processing & Packaging

Our team carefully processes and packages your items. You'll receive updates on this stage via email.

Shipment Dispatch

Your order is handed over to our logistics partner. You'll receive a shipping confirmation with tracking information.

In Transit

Track your package in real-time as it moves through the delivery network to reach you.

Out for Delivery

Your package is out for delivery. Our delivery partner will contact you before arrival.

Delivered

Your order has been successfully delivered. We hope you love your Fashion Finds!

Tracking Tips

Processing Time

Most orders are processed within 1-2 business days. During peak periods, processing may take slightly longer.

Delivery Time

Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days. Express delivery options are available at checkout.

Mobile Updates

Enable notifications in your account settings to receive real-time delivery updates on your mobile device.

Need Help?

If you're having trouble tracking your order, our customer service team is available 24/7 to assist you.

Need Assistance With Your Order?

Our customer service team is here to help with any questions about your order status.
