Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, services, and policies

Fashion Finds is your ultimate destination for trendy and affordable fashion. We offer a wide range of clothing, accessories, and footwear for men, women, and kids. Our curated collections are designed to help you express your unique style while staying within your budget.

You can reach our customer support team through multiple channels:
  • Email: support@fashionfinds.com
  • Phone: +91 98765 43210
  • Live Chat: Available on our website from 9 AM to 9 PM IST
  • Social Media: Message us on Facebook, Instagram, or Twitter
Our team typically responds within 24 hours.

Currently, we only ship within India. We're working on expanding our services to international destinations. Please check back soon for updates on international shipping options.

We offer several shipping options:
  • Standard Delivery: 5-7 business days - Free on orders over ₹999
  • Express Delivery: 2-3 business days - ₹150
  • Same-Day Delivery: Available in select cities - ₹250
Delivery times may vary based on your location and product availability.

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number and link. You can also track your order by:
  1. Logging into your account
  2. Going to "My Orders" section
  3. Selecting the order you want to track
  4. Clicking on "Track Order" button
You'll receive regular updates on your order status via email and SMS.

We offer a 30-day return policy from the date of delivery. Items must be:
  • In original condition with tags attached
  • Unused and unwashed
  • In original packaging
To initiate a return:
  1. Log into your account
  2. Go to "My Orders"
  3. Select the order and click "Return Item"
  4. Follow the instructions to print return label
Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days after we receive the item.

Refunds are typically processed within 7-10 business days after we receive the returned item. The time it takes for the refund to appear in your account depends on your payment method:
  • Credit/Debit Card: 5-7 business days
  • Net Banking: 3-5 business days
  • Wallet: 1-2 business days
You'll receive a confirmation email once the refund is processed.

We accept various payment methods for your convenience:
  • Credit and Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, Rupay)
  • Net Banking (All major banks)
  • UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.)
  • Digital Wallets (Paytm, PhonePe, Amazon Pay)
  • Cash on Delivery (COD) - Available for orders up to ₹5000
All transactions are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption.

Yes, it's completely safe. We use industry-standard security measures including:
  • 256-bit SSL encryption for all transactions
  • PCI DSS compliance for card payments
  • 3D Secure authentication for credit/debit cards
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
We do not store your complete card information on our servers. Your payment details are processed securely by our payment partners.

Creating an account is simple:
  1. Click on "Login/Register" at the top right corner
  2. Select "Create New Account"
  3. Enter your personal details (name, email, phone number)
  4. Create a password
  5. Verify your email address through the confirmation link sent to your inbox
Having an account allows you to track orders, save addresses, and access exclusive offers.

To reset your password:
  1. Click on "Login" at the top right corner
  2. Click on "Forgot Password?"
  3. Enter your registered email address
  4. Check your email for a password reset link
  5. Click the link and create a new password
The reset link is valid for 24 hours. If you don't receive the email, please check your spam folder.
