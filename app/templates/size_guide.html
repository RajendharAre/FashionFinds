How to Measure Yourself

For the most accurate sizing, follow these measurement guidelines:

Bust/Chest

Measure around the fullest part of your bust/chest, keeping the tape measure parallel to the floor.

Waist

Measure around your natural waistline, which is typically the narrowest part of your torso.

Hips

Measure around the fullest part of your hips, keeping the tape measure level.

Measurement Tips

Wear fitted clothing when measuring. Don't pull the tape measure too tight. Have someone help you if possible for the most accurate measurements.

Sizing Tips

Helpful advice to ensure you get the perfect fit:

Between Sizes?

If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for comfort, especially for items that won't be tailored.

Fabric Considerations

Consider fabric stretch and thickness when selecting your size. Stretchy materials may fit differently than rigid fabrics.

Easy Exchanges

If your item doesn't fit perfectly, our hassle-free exchange policy makes it easy to swap for a different size.

Need Help?

Our customer service team is available to help you choose the right size for any item you're considering.
