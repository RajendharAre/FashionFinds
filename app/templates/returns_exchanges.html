Returns & Exchanges

Easy returns within 30 days

Return Policy

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unused, unwashed, and in their original packaging with all tags attached.

How to Return

1. Go to My Orders and select the order you want to return.

2. Click Cancel Order and select a reason.

3. Our delivery partner will pick up the item from your address.

Exchanges

For size exchanges, please return the original item and place a new order for the desired size.

Refunds

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item. The amount will be credited to your original payment method.

Non-Returnable Items

Innerwear, swimwear, and items marked as "Final Sale" cannot be returned.
