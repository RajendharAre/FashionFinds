Return Eligibility

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. You may return most new, unopened items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

Eligible Items

Most unworn, unwashed items with original tags and packaging are eligible for return or exchange.

Final Sale Items

Certain items marked as final sale, beauty products, and intimates are not eligible for return.

Gift Items

Gift cards and promotional items are non-returnable but may be exchangeable under certain conditions.

Sale Items

Sale items may be returned for store credit or exchanged for items of equal or greater value.

Return Process

Returning an item is simple with our streamlined process:

1

Initiate Return

Log into your account and go to "My Orders". Select the order and click "Request Return".

2

Print Label

Print your prepaid return shipping label and attach it to your package.

3

Package Item

Securely package your item in its original packaging if possible.

4

Ship Return

Drop off your package at any authorized shipping location.

5

Receive Refund

Once we receive and inspect your item, we'll process your refund or exchange.

Exchanges

We offer exchanges for items that don't fit or meet your expectations:

Size Exchange

Exchange for a different size of the same item at no additional cost.

Color Exchange

Exchange for a different color of the same item style.

Style Exchange

Exchange for a different style, with price adjustments as needed.

Exchange Policy

Exchanges are subject to availability. If the desired item is out of stock, you may choose a refund or select an alternative item.

Non-Returnable Items

Certain items cannot be returned due to health, safety, or hygiene reasons:

Beauty Products

Opened cosmetics, skincare, and personal care items

Intimates

Lingerie, swimwear, and undergarments that have been worn

Gift Cards

Purchased or received gift cards and promotional codes

Altered Items

Items that have been customized, tailored, or altered

Return Conditions

To ensure your return is accepted, please follow these guidelines:

  • Original Condition: Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition
  • Tags Attached: All original tags and labels must be attached
  • Original Packaging: Items should be returned in original packaging when possible
  • Proof of Purchase: Include your order confirmation or packing slip
  • Hygiene: Items must be free from makeup, deodorant, or other stains
  • Accessories: Include all original accessories, manuals, and components

Inspection Process

All returns are inspected upon receipt. We reserve the right to deny returns that do not meet our conditions.
